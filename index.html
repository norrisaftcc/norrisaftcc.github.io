Brush-N-Tune: Revolutionizing Oral Care with Music

Executive Summary

Brush-N-Tune is an innovative oral care product that combines advanced dental hygiene technology with personalized music streaming. Our smart toothbrush syncs with Spotify, providing users with a unique, enjoyable brushing experience while collecting valuable dental health data.

Market Opportunity

The global oral care market is projected to reach $53.3 billion by 2025, with smart toothbrushes expected to see significant growth. Simultaneously, the music streaming market is booming, with over 400 million subscribers worldwide. Brush-N-Tune sits at the intersection of these two growing markets, offering a unique value proposition to tech-savvy consumers who prioritize both dental health and entertainment.

Product Features

  • Advanced Dental Scanning Technology: Collects and analyzes personal dental hygiene data.
  • Spotify Integration: Streams user's playlists directly through the toothbrush.
  • Mobile App: Provides dental health insights and manages music preferences.
  • Premium Design: Available in brushed aluminum and rose gold finishes.

Target Market

Our primary target audience includes:

  • Millennials and Gen Z consumers (ages 18-40)
  • Tech enthusiasts
  • Health-conscious individuals
  • Music lovers
